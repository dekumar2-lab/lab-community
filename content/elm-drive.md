Elm Drive West, located in the heart of Mississauga's Square One neighbourhood, is a high-density residential area known for its numerous condominium developments. 

This area offers a mix of urban and suburban lifestyles, with close proximity to the city's main attractions, shopping, and transit, making it a key part of the booming Mississauga condo market.
